What is CPS Test?

CPS is a short for Clicks Per Second and a game in which you have to click as fast as you can in a given interval. It's easy and fun at the same time. To start the game, start clicking on the area, and quickly after clicking the area start mouse-clicking as fast as you can. Once the timer ends, you will get your final score.
